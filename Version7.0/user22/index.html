WHO IS THE GOAT???

  1. When talking about GOAT, Michael Jordan is a name so accustomed to being on the top list. It’s no wonder given the performances he always shows every time NBA seasons are up. For instance, what sets him apart from most of the people on this list is his perfect NBA Finals record. With his standing of 6-0, where he dominated and won MVP in 6 Finals in two various 3-peats, this name can’t be missed.
  2. Lebron James is also a huge name in NBA’s basketball history. He’s even popular for having more game-winning buzzer-beaters during the playoffs than any player in the history of the NBA – even more than the stats of both Kobe Bryant and Michael Jordan combined. He leads the scoring among all the active players and is the only player with more than 9,000 career points in NBA history. He was called “The King” for obvious reasons, and is the player that gets the most prop bets in online NBA sportsbooks.
